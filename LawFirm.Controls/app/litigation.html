Mediation

Salha Albasti Advocates & Legal Consultants seeks to amicably settle disputes between parties whenever possible. The Firm offers an array of mediation services to assist our clients in avoiding costly litigation. Our Mediation practice includes:

  • Strategic legal advice and consultation for private settlements
  • Representation in negotiations between adverse parties
  • Drafting, execution and enforcement of Settlement Agreements
  • Representation before RERA and the Dubai Land Department for mediation of disputes involving title deed transfers, service charges, and strata law violations

Representation before Dubai Municipality and other municipal authorities for mediation of disputes involving permits, zoning regulations, easements, and other land use restrictions

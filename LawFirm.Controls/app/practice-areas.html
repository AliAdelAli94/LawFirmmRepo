Sharia Law

The basis of all legal systems in the the UAE is Sharia or Quranic Law. In the constitutions, Islam is identified as the state religion as well as the principal source of law. However, although the principles of Sharia influence criminal and civil laws, the direct influence of of Sharia in the UAE is primarily confined to social laws, such as family law, divorce or succession.
